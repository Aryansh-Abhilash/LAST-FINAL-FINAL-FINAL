Research & Innovations

Parkinson's research is evolving rapidly, with scientists exploring new treatments and potential cures. Here are some of the latest developments:

Gene Therapy

Exploring how modifying specific genes may slow or stop disease progression.

Stem Cell Therapy

Using stem cells to replace damaged neurons in the brain, showing promising results in early trials.

New Medications

Innovative drugs targeting dopamine regulation and reducing motor symptoms.
